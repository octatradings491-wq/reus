The Reuss subsea limited Titan Naming Ceremony

On Dec. 10, we celebrated our newest 8th-generation drillship—Reuss subsea limited Titan—at her official naming ceremony in Singapore! Titan is the most technically capable drilling rig in the world, equipped with two 20,000-psi BOPs, 3-million-pound hook load, and five 10,000-psi mud pumps.
